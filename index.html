Lemonade Stand – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-26

What we collect

  • Account: email (Firebase Authentication)
  • Subscriptions: purchase token & subscription status (Google Play Billing)
  • Diagnostics: crash/usage data (Firebase Crashlytics/Analytics)
  • Content you add: barcodes, notes, and photos (Firebase Storage)
  • Device access: camera for barcode scanning and item photos

Why we collect it

  • Authenticate your account & gate paid features
  • Provide inventory features (scanning & photos)
  • Process subscriptions via Google Play
  • Improve reliability & fix crashes

Sharing

No data is sold. We use service providers (Google Firebase/Play) to operate the app.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit and access is restricted via Firebase Security Rules.

Retention

We keep data while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

Data deletion

In the app: ⋮ → Delete account & data. If you can’t sign in, use our data deletion page.
